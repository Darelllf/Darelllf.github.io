What is "Bang
Dream"?

BanG Dream! (バンドリ!, Bandori!) is a Japanese multimedia project by Bushiroad, launched in January 2015 and officially announced in February 28, 2015. It began as a manga before expanding to other medias such as animes, live concerts, singles and albums, and a game. It features 4 live bands (Poppin'Party, Roselia, RAISE A SUILEN, Morfonica) and another 3 bands (Afterglow, Pastel*Palettes, and Hello, Happy World!)
